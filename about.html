About Premier Homes

Your trusted partner in the real estate market since 2008

Our Story

Founded in 2008 by real estate veterans Michael and Sarah Thompson, Premier Homes was built on the principle that buying or selling a home should be a seamless, stress-free experience guided by experts who truly care.

What began as a small family operation has grown into one of the region's most respected real estate agencies, with over 50 dedicated agents and staff serving thousands of satisfied clients.

Despite our growth, we've maintained our commitment to personalized service, ensuring that every client feels supported throughout their real estate journey.

Our Mission

"To guide our clients through the real estate process with integrity, expertise, and dedication, ensuring they find not just a property, but a place they're proud to call home."
